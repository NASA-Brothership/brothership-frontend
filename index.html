DroughtManager

Helping you protect your crops from drought

What is DroughtManager?

DroughtManager helps you understand the condition of your crops and the weather around you. Using satellite data, the app shows if your crops might suffer from drought and gives advice on what to do next.

How can it help?

With DroughtManager, you will know when your crops need water. It tells you:

  • How much water your crops need
  • When drought might happen
  • How to save your crops during dry times
